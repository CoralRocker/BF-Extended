> Format:
> Starting at start of line, Name the optimization.
> Once, named, enter a space, then '=' and another space and descibe your optimization
> There should be a file called Your_Optimization_Name.dat in the directory for this to work


NoOpt = no meaningful optimizations in place
OptSum = addition and subtraction operations concatenated to be a small as possible
OptSpace = meaningless whitespace removed from program (anything either inbetween @s, 's, or "s)
OptZero = [-] operations are shortened to *ptr = 0
OptShift = Concatenate shifting operations into one.
OptCopy = Concatenate copy operations down to one move operation
